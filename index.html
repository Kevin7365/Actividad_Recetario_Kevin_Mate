Omelette Recipe

Classic Omelette

Prep

5 mins

Cook

5 mins

Total

10 mins

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • room temperature
  • 1/4 cup whole milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs and milk until light and frothy.
  2. Heat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Add the butter.
  3. Pour in the eggs. Let them set for a few seconds.
